Key Specificity Rules

1. Inline styles (style attribute) have the highest specificity: 1,0,0,0

2. IDs have higher specificity than classes: 0,1,0,0 vs 0,0,1,0

3. Classes have higher specificity than elements: 0,0,1,0 vs 0,0,0,1

4. More specific selectors win over less specific ones

5. !important declarations override normal specificity rules

6. Equal specificity: Last rule in stylesheet wins
